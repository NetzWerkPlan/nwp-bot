1. Your nwp-bot is deployed!

- The bot listens on port 8080 by default.
- Sensitive values are stored in a Kubernetes Secret. To update them, edit your values.yaml and upgrade the release.
- The private key is mounted at /app/.data/private-key.pem.

Example to get logs:
  kubectl logs deployment/$(kubectl get deploy -l app=nwp-bot -o jsonpath='{.items[0].metadata.name}')

To test the service:
  kubectl port-forward svc/$(kubectl get svc -l app=nwp-bot -o jsonpath='{.items[0].metadata.name}') 8080:8080

If you enabled ingress, your webhook endpoint will be available at:
  http(s)://<your-ingress-host>/api/github/hook

Remember to set all required secret values in your values.yaml before installing!
